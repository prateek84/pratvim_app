About Pratvim

Curiosity with thoughtful boundaries

Pratvim is a protected AI learning space created for children and the adults who care for them. Kids can ask questions in simple language while parents manage profiles, access, screen-time habits, and safety activity.

How it helps

Answers are designed to be clear, age-aware, and encouraging. Pratvim supports text and image questions, keeps family controls in the parent experience, and guides children toward safer questions when a topic needs adult support.

Our approach

Pratvim supports learning, not unsupervised decision-making. Parents remain in control of child access and can review activity whenever needed.

Privacy policy

Family privacy comes first

Pratvim collects only the account and profile information needed to operate the family experience, such as a parent email, child profile name, age range, and app activity.

Child information

Child profiles are managed by the parent account. Private PINs are shown only inside parent editing tools and are never displayed on Parent Home.

Conversations

Conversation data may be stored to let a child continue learning and to provide parents with safety and activity summaries. It is not presented as public content.

Your choices

Parents can update profile details, remove child profiles, and request account or stored-data deletion through support.

Terms & Conditions

Using Pratvim as a family

Pratvim is a protected learning service for children under the supervision of a parent or guardian. A parent account is responsible for creating child profiles, managing access, and keeping account details accurate.

Parent consent

By creating an account, the parent or guardian confirms that they are authorized to provide consent for each child profile and will supervise how the service is used.

Appropriate use

Pratvim may be used for age-appropriate learning and curiosity. Accounts must not be used to harm others, bypass safety controls, share unlawful content, or attempt to access another family's information.

Subscriptions

Paid plans apply to one parent account and up to four child profiles. Plan duration, renewal details, and remaining access are shown in the parent experience before a payment is confirmed.

AI answers

AI responses can be incomplete or incorrect and do not replace a parent, teacher, doctor, or other qualified professional. Children should ask a trusted adult before acting on important information.
